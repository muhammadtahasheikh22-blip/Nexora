Frequently Asked Questions

Our rates are fetched from the Open Exchange Rates API and are updated in real-time. The rates are indicative and may differ slightly from bank rates or the rates offered by money transfer services. For financial transactions, always verify with your bank.
Nexora's currency converter supports 150+ world currencies including all major currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, CHF) as well as regional currencies like PKR (Pakistani Rupee), INR (Indian Rupee), AED (UAE Dirham), SAR (Saudi Riyal), and many more.
Exchange rates are updated every hour. Click the "Refresh Rates" button to get the latest rates. Note that currency markets are closed on weekends, so Friday's closing rates are used on Saturdays and Sundays.
The current PKR to USD rate is displayed live in our converter above. Select PKR as the "From" currency and USD as the "To" currency to see the latest rate.
