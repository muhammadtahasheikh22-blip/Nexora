Frequently Asked Questions

This tool uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API to convert text to audio. It works completely in your browser — no data is sent to any server. The available voices depend on your operating system and browser.
The available voices and languages depend on your device. Most modern browsers support English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Hindi, Urdu, and many more languages through installed system voices.
The Web Speech API plays audio directly in your browser. For downloading audio files, you would need a server-based TTS service. Our Pro plan includes downloadable audio generation.

💡 Tips

• Add commas for natural pauses

• Use periods for longer stops

• Adjust speed for clarity

• Try different voices for best results
