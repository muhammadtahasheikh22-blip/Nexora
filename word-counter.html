Frequently Asked Questions

How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is calculated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute. So a 1,000-word article takes about 5 minutes to read.
Does it save my text?
No. Your text stays entirely in your browser and is never sent to any server. We respect your privacy completely.
What's the maximum text length?
There is no limit! You can paste entire books or documents and the word counter will handle them without any issues.
Can I use this for SEO content?
Yes! Use it to check your article word count, keyword density, and make sure your content meets SEO requirements for length.
